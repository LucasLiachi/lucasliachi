## Introduction to IT Governance

Technology governance is a critical component of corporate governance, ensuring that IT resources are aligned with organizational strategy and objectives. In this article, we explore the core principles of IT governance, including value delivery, risk management, resource management, and performance measurement.

### Key Frameworks

Organizations typically use frameworks like COBIT, ITIL, and ISO/IEC 38500 to structure their governance models. These frameworks help in:
- Establishing clear roles and responsibilities.
- Defining processes for decision-making and accountability.
- Aligning technology investments with business objectives.

### Conclusion

Effective technology governance leads to better risk mitigation, optimized resource utilization, and ultimately, sustainable competitive advantage.
